About Us

"We Deliver World-class Farming Solutions and High-quality Services to Food Companies."

AGRO prides itself on crops farming with excellent quality in every seed. We're focused on food and nutrition companies around the world, as well as feed producers. Our strategy is to innovate and grow our portfolio of field crops. Our current portfolio combined with technology and bioscience capability have given us a stable position in sorghum, wheat, corn, sunflower, canola, rice, etc.
