About Us

"We provide high-quality services to high-end food processing companies in industrial areas."

AGC covers crops from their origins, ensuring the richness of their skins. We focus on companies producing food and supplements. Our strategy is based on innovation and the expansion of greenhouse crops. Our current portfolio combines technological and biotechnological potential, ensuring a stable position in crops such as strawberries, blueberries, porcini mushrooms, raspberries, and others.

Blueberries

Blueberries are well known for their deep color, refreshing taste, and high antioxidant content. At AGRO, we grow carefully selected blueberry varieties that produce firm, flavorful berries with excellent nutritional value. Our blueberries are cultivated with great attention to quality and sustainability to preserve their natural sweetness and freshness. In this section, we will introduce the blueberry varieties we grow and explain their unique characteristics and benefits.

Raspberries

Raspberries are valued for their delicate flavor, pleasant sweet-and-tart taste, and rich vitamin content. At AGRO, we grow several raspberry varieties that differ in aroma, berry size, and harvesting season. We pay special attention to quality and natural growing methods to ensure that every berry maintains its freshness and natural benefits. In this section, you will learn more about the types of raspberries we grow, their features, and their advantages.

Strawberries

Strawberries are one of the most popular berries thanks to their sweet taste, juiciness, and rich aroma. At AGRO, we carefully select strawberry varieties known for their large berries, vibrant color, and high nutritional value. Our strawberries are grown following strict agricultural standards to preserve their natural freshness and flavor. In this section, we will talk about the different strawberry varieties we grow, their characteristics, and their advantages.
